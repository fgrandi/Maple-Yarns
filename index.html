About Us 💕

Hi there! I'm Stella, and this is my precious orange kitty, Maple! 🧡 We spend our days creating the most wonderful crochet projects together. Maple loves to "help" by sitting on my yarn, purring softly, and occasionally batting at the crochet hook (which makes everything take twice as long, but twice as fun!).

Every stitch is made with love, every project tells a story, and every creation is infused with Maple's magical purrs. From tiny amigurumi animals to cozy blankets, we pour our hearts into everything we make. Welcome to our little corner of the crafting world! 🌟
